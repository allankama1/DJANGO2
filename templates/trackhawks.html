TRACKHAWKS

The Grand Cherokee Trackhawk was born in 2017, during the New York International Auto Show. Jeep decided to team up with Hellcat V8 to create a powerful SUV that could not only conquer the roads but also blast through the racetracks. Sporting a monstrous 6.2L Hellcat HEMI V8 engine, this beast churned out a whopping 707 horsepower and 645 lb-ft of torque. From its initial release in 2018, the Trackhawk saw new model productions each year. But like a fine wine, it didn't change too much, remaining mostly the same in terms of performance and appearance.
